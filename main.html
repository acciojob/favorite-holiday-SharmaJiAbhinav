JAMMU KASHMIR

Between the green hills, streams, and meadows lie a valley In Jammu and Kashmir's Kupwara where the cattle run wild, birds sing lullabies through the day.[citation needed] The place is named Bangus Valley. According to a report,[clarification needed] the valley is located nearly 10,000 meters above sea level (MSL). The Bangus Valley is located in Kupwara district of North Kashmir. It is situated to the West of Handwara town along the Mawri River within the Handwara sub-district. district Kupwara. The term Bangus comes from Sanskrit word Van (Forest) and gus (grass).The valley is nearly 130 km from Srinagar and is situated on the northwestern border of Tehsil Handwara in Kupwara.
